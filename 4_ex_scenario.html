Example Scenario


Here is an example scenario with sample responses:

Scenario: the autopilot of your car is now on and the car is driving itself safely on the highway towards your destination. You are interested in checking and responding to recent text messages.

Here is an image of the in-vehicle user interface:




Sample Task 1:

Now, which input command would result in the following user interface image:




Examples of how to respond:


1. Using a voice command, what you would tell the autonomous vehicle to do?


2. Using a gesture, how would you complete this task?


We will then ask you, which of these command modalities you prefer and why.


Important: For the purpose of this study, assume that the autopilot drives the car safely - the car's automation system knows how to handle all emergency situations. Also, assume that the voice input will always understand you and that the gestures you make in the car will always be recognized by the system.


We know that your responses will be speculative and we are looking forward to seeing your ideas!
